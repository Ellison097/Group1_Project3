Research Output Analysis

A comprehensive analysis of FSRDC research outputs

Publication Trends

Analysis

The analysis of publication trends reveals significant growth in research output from 2000 to 2023, with clear peaks in recent years. The data shows that RDCs such as Michigan, Boston, Chicago, and Triangle are particularly productive, indicating strong institutional support and collaborative environments.

Citation Analysis

Analysis

Citation distributions show heavy skewness with most publications having low citation counts, while a small subset has exceptionally high citations. The analysis by RDC reveals that institutions like Washington, USC, and Berkeley excel in producing highly cited research.

Topic Modeling

Analysis

Topic modeling reveals several dominant research themes, including economic policy, market analysis, and health research. The analysis shows how these themes have evolved over time, with particular growth in technology-related topics in recent years.

Collaboration Networks

Analysis

The collaboration network analysis identifies key researchers and institutions that serve as central nodes in the research ecosystem. This analysis helps understand how knowledge flows through the network and identifies potential collaboration opportunities.
